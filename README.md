# CS230-Project4-Group6-Mindful AI 

At MindfulAI, we aim to deliver professional grade psychological support at affordability and scale. We are developing a global mental health companion powered by AI, delivered at scale to support people in managing mental wellness. This AI powered mental health companion will incorporate evidence-based techniques from CBT and support for crisis intervention. It will also enable users to monitor their mental wellness over time. Our main goals are to close the global treatment gap for mental health by providing access to mental health support for people with no current access, to support traditional therapists as a useful companion in between sessions, to act as a gateway for those afraid of traditional therapy, and to deliver AI powered mental health support that is built with strong ethical considerations, that is clinically validated, and that securely protects user privacy.
